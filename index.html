How to Play

Catch falling stars with your basket to score points!

You have 3 lives - lose a life for each star you miss.

Controls

  • PC/Laptop: Use A/D or Left/Right arrow keys to move the basket
  • Mobile: Tap on the left or right side of the screen to move

The game gets faster as you score more points!

Try to beat your high score and make it to the leaderboard!
